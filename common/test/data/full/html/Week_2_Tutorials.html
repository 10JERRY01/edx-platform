Week 2 Tutorials

Basic Tutorials

  • Is it linear? - We step through a set of common circuit elements (including several not yet seen in lecture), and let you try to guess whether they are linear or nonlinear.
  • How good is the lightbulb model? - We measure a real lightbulb, and see how well a resistor models it.
  • Superposition - We solve the circuit from last week's tutorial using superposition, and see how much faster this is.

Worked Problems

  • OCW Ex 3.2/AL Exercise 3.2 parts a-d - We go from a truth table to logic gates to NAND gates
  • Thevenin Example - We go through an example of the Thevenin circuit.
  • Thevenin Example 2 - A different way of looking at Thevenin.

Bonus Material

  • Speakers, heatsinks, and houses - We show how to apply electrical models to understanding homemade speakers and insulating houses.
